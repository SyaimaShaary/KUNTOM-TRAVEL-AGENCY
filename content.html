MALAYSIA PACKAGES


Discover Malaysia with Kuntom Travel Agency



At Kuntom Travel Agency, we offer a diverse range of travel packages tailored to showcase the best of Malaysia.
From the bustling streets of Kuala Lumpur to the pristine beaches of Langkawi, our packages cover all major attractions and hidden gems.

Cultural Tours

Customizable Packages

Immerse yourself in Malaysia's rich history and diverse cultures. Visit iconic landmarks like the Petronas Twin Towers,
explore the historic streets of Malacca, and experience the vibrant local markets.

For nature enthusiasts, our packages include trips to the lush rainforests of Borneo, the stunning Cameron Highlands, and
the breathtaking Taman Negara. Discover unique wildlife and enjoy activities like hiking and river cruises.

Relax on the beautiful beaches of Langkawi, Perhentian, and Tioman islands. Enjoy water sports, snorkeling, and
diving in crystal-clear waters.

We also offer customizable packages to suit your preferences. Whether you're traveling solo, as a couple, or with family,
we can create an itinerary that matches your interests and budget.
